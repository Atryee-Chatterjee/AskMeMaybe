How AskMeMaybe Works

Retrieval-Augmented Generation in three powerful steps

1. Upload & Process

Upload your PDFs. Our system extracts text page-by-page using PyPDF, then splits content into semantic chunks (1000 chars with 200 overlap). Each chunk is converted into vector embeddings using sentence-transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2).

2. Semantic Search

When you ask a question, we convert it to an embedding and perform similarity search against your document chunks in the FAISS vector database. The system retrieves the top 4 most relevant chunks along with metadata (filename and page number).

3. Generate Answer

The retrieved chunks are fed as context into Llama 3.1 (8B Instruct) via Hugging Face. The LLM generates accurate, grounded answers based ONLY on your documents. Every response includes citations showing exactly which source and page the information came from.
