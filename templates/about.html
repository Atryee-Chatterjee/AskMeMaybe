How AskMeMaybe Works

Retrieval-Augmented Generation in three powerful steps

1. Upload & Process

Upload your PDFs (max 2MB each). Our system extracts text page-by-page using PyPDF, then splits content into optimized semantic chunks (400 chars with 50 overlap). Each chunk is stored in a FAISS vector database for fast retrieval.

2. Semantic Search

When you ask a question, we perform a similarity search against your document chunks in the FAISS vector database. The system retrieves the top 3 most relevant chunks along with metadata (filename and page number).

3. Generate Answer

The retrieved chunks are fed as context into Llama 3.1 (8B Instruct) via OpenRouter. The LLM generates accurate, grounded answers based ONLY on your documents. Every response includes clickable citations showing exactly which source and page the information came from.
